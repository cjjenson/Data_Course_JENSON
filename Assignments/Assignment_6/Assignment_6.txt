 1. Which religious group correlates most strongly in a given area with the proportion of non-religious people?

The strongest correlation is LDS with a negative correlation of 0.7565. 

# 2.  What is the direction of that correlation?

Negative (downward)

# 3.  Which religious group has the second strongest correlation, as above?

Episcopal Church with 0.3557

# 4.  What is the direction of THAT correlation?

Postive (Upward)

# 5.  What can you say about these relationships?

We can't say that there is any causation, but it appears as the number of non-religious individuals goes up the percentage of LDS goes down while the Episcopal Church members goes up.
